EDITING

The Future of VFX and Post Production

Some of the areas most affected by AI innovation are editing, VFX, and post production. While machines cannot fully replace human creativity just yet, similar concerns are shared between artists, graphic designers, editors, and more. What used to take one person hours to remove objects or backgrounds, can seamlessly be done in one click with new AI tools.

Adobe Premiere Pro

A leading video-editing software, Premiere Pro offers a handful of AI instruments for creators. Tools such as Adobe Sensei powered enhancements allow editors to automatically color correct and improve the appearance of their footage. Others, like "Enhance Speech" clean up audio files through AI technologies.

TRY IT OUT

Runway

A company pushing the power of creativity through AI innovation, Runway can not only generate footage but provides tools for editing as well. Objects and background can seamlessly be removed, motion tracking can be enhanced, and there are even sophisticated greenscreen features. However, the software is best known for its text-to-video AI models, which are considered to be some of the best in the business. Even editors from the Oscar-winning film "Everything Everywhere All at Once" utilized Runway to edit scenes.

TRY IT OUT

Vidyo AI

A content repurposing tool, used mainly by creators and businesses, helps to select the best short-form content from long-form videos. While not a leading software in Hollywood, its capabilities in the editing realm save hours of work for content creators, such as podcasters and YouTubers.
